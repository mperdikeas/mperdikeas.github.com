Reporting Requirements (FATCA / FBAR) and their forms

  • FinCEN Form 114 — FBAR
    • This form is filed independently of the Income Tax Return but observes the same deadlines.
  • FATCA Form 8938
    • This form is part of the Income Tax Return.

Income forms

  • Form 1040 — U.S. Individual Income Tax Return: this is the main income tax form
  • Form 8621 — income from a PFIC/QEF (a PFIC will typically send a "PFIC Annual Information Statement" containing such information as is necessary to fill Form 8621).

Acronyms

FATCA
Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act
FBAR
Foreign Bank Account Report
FinCEN
Financial Crimes Enforcement Network
PFIC
Passive Foreign Investment Company
QEF
Qualified Electing Fund
